Deforestation Impacts on ecosystems?

One of the major impacts of deforestation is the loss of habitat, the most direct impact for many species, the distruction of thier shelter, food and breeding growns can kill thousands and those that do struggle. This can go as far a disrupting the food web and species extinction due to their lack of adapting.
