Habits

To help combat the negative impacts of deforestation and bring change to our environment, you as an individual implement some daily habits, these can be big or small, this can just mean looking to purchase FSC wood to make sure that even when buying wood it is the best possible wood, or you could just cut down on your use of wood products, this could include paper, furniture, and building materials, this doesent mean you cant have wood product but you could use the option for alternatives like recycled paper, bamboo furniture, and other sustainable materials.
